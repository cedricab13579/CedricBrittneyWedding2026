Our Love Story

How we found each other

How We Met

We've known each other for 17 years, stretching waaaaay back to third grade, where we were fast friends. It took Cedric only 8-ish more years to muster the courage, asking for a first date in December 2016, where our love story begins. From there we've been inseparable, through long distance in undergrad to living together as Brittney completes her doctorate.

The Proposal

In February 2025 after 8 years of dating, Cedric and Brittney were finally ready to take the next step in their lives together. After an exciting day of falconry on a gorgeous winter day, Cedric and Brittney came home to a warm home-cooked meal where through happy tears, she said yes!
