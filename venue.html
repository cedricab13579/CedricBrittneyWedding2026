About Hollyfield Manor

Nestled in the heart of Richmond, Virginia, Hollyfield Manor stands as a testament to timeless elegance and Southern charm. This magnificent antebellum mansion, with its iconic white columns and stately facade, has been the backdrop for countless love stories and unforgettable celebrations.

Historic Elegance

Built in the early 1800s, Hollyfield Manor combines historic grandeur with modern amenities. The manor's classic Greek Revival architecture features towering white columns, symmetrical design, and traditional brick construction that exemplifies the finest of Southern architectural heritage.

Perfect Setting

The manor's expansive front lawn provides a breathtaking ceremony space with the stately mansion as your backdrop. Mature oak trees frame the property, while meticulously maintained gardens and classic brick walkways create an atmosphere of romance and sophistication.

Ceremony & Reception

The front lawn can accommodate up to 200 guests for your ceremony, with the manor's grand facade providing a stunning backdrop for your vows. The elegant interior offers sophisticated reception areas with crystal chandeliers, hardwood floors, and period furnishings.

Modern Amenities

While preserving its historic charm, Hollyfield Manor has been carefully updated with modern conveniences including climate control, professional lighting systems, full catering facilities, and ample parking to ensure your celebration is both beautiful and comfortable.
